BenjaminPop! Scratch-Off Greeting Cards

Design & Illustration

BenjaminPop! was looking for a fun and unique greeting card that harmonizes with the style of the stationery brand.

What was created was a bold and playful card inspired by the game "never have I ever" with a hidden message under each scratch-off area, along with notepads in the same style.

This card received "Best New Product" nomination at the 2017 National Stationery Show in New York.
